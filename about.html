About Me

👋 Hey there!

I’m Shreya Vontela — a data scientist who loves turning messy data into meaningful, intelligent stories. I started in finance and fell in love with machine learning, where math meets creativity.

These days, I build ML models, GenAI systems, and explore Agentic AI — teaching machines to reason and act a little more like humans (the nice kind 😄).

I’ve worn quite a few hats — from modeling credit losses at JP Morgan, to automating systems at Bank of America, to designing new-age AI pipelines. I love taking abstract ideas, playing with data, and shipping things that make a difference.

🚀 What Keeps Me Going

I’m a lifelong learner who believes the best solutions happen when tech, intuition, and curiosity come together. When I’m not experimenting with models, I’m doodling product ideas, reading about the future of AI agents, or chasing my next “what if…?” moment.
