About Me

👋 Hey there!

I’m Shreya Vontela — a data scientist who loves turning messy data into meaningful, intelligent stories. I started my career crunching numbers in finance and slowly fell in love with the world of machine learning, where math meets creativity.

These days, I work on building ML models, GenAI systems, and exploring the exciting space of Agentic AI — teaching machines to think and act a little more like humans (the nice kind 😄).

I’ve worn quite a few hats — from modeling credit losses at JP Morgan, to automating systems at Bank of America, to designing new-age AI pipelines. I love the feeling of taking an abstract idea, playing with data, and building something that actually makes a difference.

🚀 What Keeps Me Going

I’m a lifelong learner who believes the best solutions happen when tech, intuition, and curiosity come together. When I’m not experimenting with models, you’ll probably find me doodling product ideas, reading about the future of AI agents, or chasing my next big “what if…?” moment.
